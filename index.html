Dr. Thomas White

I'm an evolutionary biologist, and have a particular interest in sensory ecology and communication. I mostly work with invertebrates for their tractability in addressing questions of evolution and behaviour, and for the unique opportunities for discovery offered by non-model organisms. In 2018 I joined the wonderful School of Life and Environmental Sciences at The University of Sydney as a lecturer and Early Career Development Fellow.
