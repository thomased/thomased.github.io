Dr. Thomas White

I'm an evolutionary biologist, and have a particular interest in sensory ecology and communication. I mostly work with invertebrates for their experimental tractability in addressing questions of evolution and behaviour, and for the opportunities afforded by non-model organisms. Since 2018 I've been a lecturer in the wonderful School of Life and Environmental Sciences at The University of Sydney.
