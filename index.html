--- layout: page dispname: Evolutionary and Sensory Ecology --- Dr. Thomas White I'm an evolutionary biologist, and have a particular interest in sensory ecology and communication. I mostly work with invertebrates for their experimental tractability in addressing questions of evolution and behaviour, and for the opportunities afforded by non-model organisms. I've been a research fellow in Darrell Kemp's lab at Macquarie University since receiving my PhD earlier this year, and have close ties to the Umbers lab at Western Sydney University.
